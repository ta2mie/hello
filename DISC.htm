DISC 人格測試: 分析個人的行為特徵，幫助人們更好地了解自己和他人
DISC Personality Test: Understand your personality type and how you behave around others

請選擇最符合自己的答案，然後點擊"提交"按鈕，查看測試結果。
Choose the answer that best fits you for each question, then click "Submit" to see your results.

1. 你在同學/同事眼中是怎樣的？ How do your classmates/colleagues see you?




2. 你喜歡看哪一類型的雜誌？ What kind of magazines do you like reading?




3. 你做決策的方式？ How do you make decisions?




4. 你最擅長哪種工作？ What kind of work suits you best?




5. 當面對壓力時，你會？ How do you react when under pressure?




6. 與同學/同事之間的相處？ How do you get along with classmates/colleagues?




7. 你希望別人如何與你溝通？ How do you like people to talk to you?




8. 要完成一件事情時，你最在意的部分是？ When doing a task, what matters most to you?




9. 甚麼事情會讓你恐懼？ What worries you the most?




10. 你自覺有甚麼缺點？ What would you say is your weakness?
